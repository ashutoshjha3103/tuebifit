1 · The market problem

Beginners

  • Program uncertainty: New users struggle to pick the right plan and execute exercises correctly.
  • Fragmented guidance: Advice is scattered across apps and creators, increasing confusion and injury risk.
Impact: Early churn in weeks 1-8

Intermediate & advanced

  • Nutrition mismatch: Calorie and macro targets often fail to match body-composition goals.
  • Programming drift: Splits and muscle focus are misaligned with equipment, recovery capacity, and priorities.
Impact: Stalled progress despite consistency

3 · Our Solution

A mobile app where an AI agent builds personalized workout + meal plans from real exercise (MCP) & nutrition DBs (MCP), along with on-device assistance.

Why Us?

  • Grounded and contextualized — tool-backed scientific data, not vibes-only convo or chat
  • Highly credible data sources
  • Unified — provide a one stop solution for training planning+ meal planning + form monitoring in one flow
  • A B2B2C wedge — consumer app with B2B pilot path (gyms, wellness platforms) for faster growth and moats
